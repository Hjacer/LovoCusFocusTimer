# LovoCus Focus Timer

**One tap. Zero friction. Total focus.**

LovoCus is an offline-first Android focus companion built for people who are tired of bloated productivity apps. No sign-ups. No subscriptions. No cloud. Just tap and enter a focused state immediately — with curated ambient soundtracks, smart session timing, and a strict accountability mode that helps you stay on track while you work.

## Why LovoCus feels different

- **One-tap focus sessions** — No setup, no decisions. Open the app and you're already in a session.
- **Curated soundscapes** — Separate ambient tracks for focus periods and break periods, so your audio environment matches your mental state.
- **Soft & Strict modes** — Soft lets you build confidence; Strict adds real accountability with interruption tracking and confirmation on exit.
- **Your personal dashboard** — Track focus time, completions, interruptions, and trends over 7 and 30 days — all computed locally.
- **Truly offline & private** — Everything lives on your device. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking SDKs, no internet required.
